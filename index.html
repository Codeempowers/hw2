Our History

When people hear Kashmir, they typically think of wool.
Think again!
As the most Northern part of Inida and overlooked by the Himalayas, Kashmir is a place of beautiful simplicity and pristine natural beauty.
In this special place we sustainably have been growing apples in our orachard for over 100 years.
We offer a variety of apple products fresh from our orchard directly to your home
